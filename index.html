About Us

We truly believe that the dog is a man's best friend. Therefore, we want to offer you the opportunity to rent a dog for your specific purpose. Rent a Friend works with a number of certified and specialized trainers, and can help you or your company with your specific need.


We often work with retired police dogs, trained search dogs, and support dogs who love to say hi at the elder homes, hospitals and day cares. Together we can make every day more fun for both dog and human.


We also have a partnership with Hundstallet who helps us find a friend for those who need some extra company. Many elders have contacted us during Covid-19 in order to get some company during these lonely times.
